About

I'm a data engineer who likes the unglamorous part of the job: making data move reliably from one place to another, then making it move faster and cheaper. Most of my work lives in migration and platform engineering — taking legacy on-prem systems and rebuilding their data flows on Google Cloud.

Lately I've paired classic pipeline work with GenAI, using models like Gemini to automate discovery and migration tasks that used to take weeks. I also build the accelerators, schemas and governance features that keep large migration programs honest — metadata, audit trails, approvals and alerting.

I currently lead and mentor a small team at Accenture while staying hands-on with the code.
